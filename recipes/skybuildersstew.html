Skybuilder's Stew

Description

One unfortunate rail was left to stew in its own juices after finding its way into the pot. Literally.

EXP Bonus: +3% Duration: 30m

(Duration can be extended to 60m by consuming multiple servings)

Ingredients

  • 4 Fire Crystal
  • 2 Water Crystal
  • 8 Skybuilders' Wheat
  • 8 Skybuilders' Highland Spring Water
  • 8 Skybuilders' Sea Salt
  • 2 Rail Tenderloin

Steps

  1. Add Rail Tenderloin to large pot.
  2. Use Fire Crystals to cook until brown. About 8 minutes per side.
  3. Remove Rail Tenderloin from pot and set aside.
  4. Add garlic and onions to pot, scrape up fond with released liquid.
  5. Add butter and flour to make roux.
  6. Add water to pot and stir. Make sure there are no lumps.
  7. Add seasonings and bring to simmer.
  8. Prepare dough dumplings.
  9. Cut up Rail Tenderloin and throw back into pot.
  10. Add dumplings and any other vegetables/seasonings.
  11. Cover pot and cook 15 minutes.
